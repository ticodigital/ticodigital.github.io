About Me

I'm Louis D. Baldioceda Méndez, a Costa Rican (aka tico) financial data scientist solving complex problems using data-driven solutions. I specialize in Financial Planning & Analysis, Data Engineering, and Business Intelligence. Leveraging technologies such as Power BI, Salesforce CRM, SAP BPC, Oracle Hyperion, TensorFlow, Spark, and Kafka, I deliver insights that drive decisions and operational efficiencies.
